The Main Idea of this Website

Keman Singh | 18, October, 2018

 

The big fact is that education is itself a form of propaganda – a deliberate scheme to outfit the pupil, not with the capacity to weigh ideas but with a simple appetite for gulping ideas ready-made. The aim is to make ‘good’ citizens which is to say, docile and uninquisitive citizens. – H.L. Mencken

The above quote may seem like a cliche but it has persisted as the brutal truth since the dawn of time. Across the globe, technology and active learning have replaced the orthodox teaching of yester-years. But in India, we are still following the same education curriculum which was set by British to use us in industries. Take the example of engineering.
